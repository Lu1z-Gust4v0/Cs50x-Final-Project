What's CryptoQuiz?

CryptoQuiz is a platform developed with the purpose of giving people basic understanding about the crypto world, while they have fun solving a quiz related to the cryptoverse.

We are going to talk about 5 different topics related to the cryptoverse: Cryptocurrencies, Blockchain, Wallets, Smart Contrats, and Web 3.0. When you start reading through the articles you will realise that all these topics are somehow related.

Quiz

You will have to solve a quiz about the topics covered in the learning section. The ideia behind this task is to test your learning. Good lucky!!
